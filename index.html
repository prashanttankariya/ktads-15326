About KTADS

Founded in 2005 by Ar. Ashish Pandya, Kruti The Architectural Design Studio (KTADS) is a multidisciplinary firm specializing in Architecture, Landscape, Interior, and Furniture Design. Our approach blends functionality, culture, and nature to create meaningful spaces. At KTADS, design goes beyond aesthetics — it shapes environments, enriches lives, and reflects our passion for timeless, sustainable creation.
